About Remote Rehab Association

Welcome to the Remote Rehab Association (RRA) website! We are a not-for-profit health clinic that specializes in providing physical rehabilitation services to individuals who do not have traditional health insurance.

At RRA, we understand that cost and travel can be significant barriers to accessing the care you need. That's why we're committed to providing affordable, accessible, and convenient rehabilitation services to our patients. Our team of licensed physical therapists is dedicated to helping you recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve your overall health and wellness.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live their best life, free from pain and limitation. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or looking to improve your athletic performance, RRA is here to help. Our in-home rehabilitation services allow you to receive the care you need in the comfort and privacy of your own home.

Thank you for considering RRA for your rehabilitation needs. We look forward to helping you achieve your health and wellness goals!
